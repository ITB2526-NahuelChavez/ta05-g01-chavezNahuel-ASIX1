Institut tecnològic de Barcelona
The ITB has strong ties to the tech-digital ecosystem in the Barcelona metropolitan area and a strong desire to meet the need to train, together with companies in the sector, competent, certified students up to date on the latest technological trends to satisfy job demands. The internship model entails closer collaboration between professional academic training and the workplace, while also allowing for specific training to be provided by the company.
